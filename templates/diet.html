Healthy Diet Plan Calculator

Online dieting calculator will help you to calculate BMI, calorie needs per day, ideal body weight and weight status based on current age, height, weight and daily activities for your healthy life.



            Formula:

            Body Mass Index(BMI) =W/(H*H)
            Ideal Weight =(0.5 * BMI +11.5)*H*H
            Calorie Needs per Day =66.67+(13.75*W)+(5*H)-(6.76*age)

            where,  H= Height
                         W= Weight
